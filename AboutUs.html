About us

Yuva Marathi (Marathi Literary Association) is a Literary and Cultural Club at Vellore Institute of Technology in Tamil Nadu. Having been established in 2016, we represent the magnificent culture and language of Maharashtra and pride on being a ‘Home away from Home’ for all Marathi students. In spite of being founded in Tamil Nadu, we have 150+ enthusiastic members functioning in nine departments working towards the fulfilment of our motto – ‘To promote Marathi culture and provide a platform to the young girls and boys for showcasing their talents and skills.’

Domains

Editorial

Being a literary association, our editorial department is the very soul of our club. Right from drama scripts and Instagram captions to publishing our very own yearly magazine, the members of this department are always ready, pen in hand, to come up with stimulating content for all of Yuva Marathi’s endeavours.

Design

The hard work and efforts of our design department determine how Yuva Marathi is perceived by our audience, especially after the world has moved online. Designing and editing Instagram posts, YouTube videos, event posters, reels, brochures, websites and so much more, the rest of our domains and academies just wouldn’t function without them.

Management

Our biggest division, the management department is the thread that binds together all different units of Yuva Marathi. Communicating between various departments, ensuring the smooth running of our events as well as managing PR and sponsorship, all are taken care of by the members of this all-encompassing division.

Technical

Being a cultural club, our technical department is surely our most unique facet. Right from web dev to organising our event – ‘Globomatics’, this truly the department for all things technical. During the raging pandemic, members of this department also helped coordinate the supply of covid resources to those in need.

Documentation

This department of ours is responsible for the keeping of records of all of Yuva Marathi’s meetings, events, and other endeavours. Its members also taking care of the submission of reports and documents to the college authorities, thus making it an extremely important department.

Academies

Drama

Be it Shivajallosh or Gudhipadwa, students of our drama academy always manage to come up with amazing dramatic presentations that never fail to entertain our audience. Their performances tie together the various aspects of the entire event and along with it the acts organized by the music and dance academies.

Dance

The members of the music academy showcase their musical skills during each of our events and are extremely instrumental in setting the tone of the occasion. When in college, our music academy holds fun jamming sessions every weekend, which are open for all, but since the global shift online, they too have shifted to YouTube.

Music

Perhaps our most exciting academy, the members of the dance academy always put up an exciting show and raise the spirits of all those watching on. During the Aikya parade, thousands line the streets, craning their neck to get a glimpse of the series of indigenous Maharashtrian dance performances that they present.
